## Bubbles launches on mainnet, farmers are relentless

![[Pasted image 20250428205743.png|150]]

@Bubbles2Earn - the dApp that promotes using a refillable bottle instead of a single-use-one, has successfully launched on mainnet. It's passport system was seriously tested instantly. And it held up. 

Throughout the week, bad actors have attacked Bubbles backend systems continuously. Rewards distributions were triggered at a few points, maxing out user's submissions (with valid passports only), and forcing Bubbles to take down the dApp. Rewards were never sent out to bad actors. 

Following that, the dApp has been taken offline here and there but all undistributed rewards are saved and will be distributed later. 

Great work Bubbles team, keep it up. Bad actors are not winning.

Source: https://x.com/Bubbles2Earn/status/1914085871319384115 & https://x.com/Bubbles2Earn/status/1916407143965618390

## Green Commuter is soon updating in a major way

![[Pasted image 20250428213006.png]]

Soon, @green_commuter is updating their dApp in a few major ways:

Great work team, looking forward to seeing all of this being implemented.
