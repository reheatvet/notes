## EVEarn introduces in-app staking and B3TR withdrawals

![[Pasted image 20250421204823.png]]

After a quiet period, @EvearnB3TR has released two updates from the recent weeks:
- B3TR withdrawals for Smart Accounts are now live
- In-app staking is integrated into their dApp. Just like many other dApps, this integration allows users to vote for their dApp automatically with @Vedelegate.

Source: https://x.com/EvearnB3TR/status/1912137018609377744

## Restify teases their native app flow

![[Pasted image 20250421205458.png]]

@Restify_App is an upcoming project on the #VeBetterDAO that promotes users to rest - to turn off, to live in the moment, to remove your phone's distractions and get rewarded for it.

It's not released yet but they've teased a nice looking flow of how the native apps (Android / iOS) might function. Looks good, and I'm sure a lot of users will appreciate the extra incentive to stop scrolling. 

Source: https://x.com/Restify_App/status/1912148890335293461

## VeStation and VeSwap gets removed from the DAO

Following a successful proposal (see source) by @MrWhittleman, @VeStation\_ and @veswaporg\_ have been removed from the DAO. 

Shortly after being removed, VeSwap published their internal report on their side of the situation. MrWhittleman and others found issues with their report, however, and VeSwap proceeded to remove their report from X. No response from VeSwap has been published since. 

Source: https://governance.vebetterdao.org/proposals/16952850511191752820402247063670202238339807172273153527040168494523752166758 & https://x.com/MrWhittleman/status/1912552246468018177

## EcoTransit gets endorsed then vanishes, Carbonlarity enters grace

EcoTransit (https://notesfromthedao.com/%E2%AD%90-Getting-Started-with-dApps#-ecotransit), the public transport dApp that has been looking for endorsements for quite a few weeks suddenly got endorsed by a MjolnirX-node. The very same that just before that removed its endorsement on @Carbonlarity, leaving them in grace.

With no social presence (X, telegram, discord) known and no known team behind it, I expressed concerns around the project when it got endorsed here https://x.com/ReheatVet/status/1913202732380594642.

It was looking like the dApp would be starting to receive votes on round 43 (starting April 21st), but they were suddenly removed from the DAO, just shortly before round 42 concluded. Hopefully an announcement can be made about what happened soon.

Source: https://governance.vebetterdao.org/apps

#ecotransit #carbonlarity

## The GM NFT proposal's next steps

The successful proposal to revamp the GM NFT system (https://governance.vebetterdao.org/proposals/29105190577567483236613349157882547619640909716326747339146460525546041993191) has gotten an update from @vechainofficial. 

The changes will be implemented but a one-month delay has been added to the GM NFT tier release schedule. That means the next GM NFT, Jupiter, will be released in May instead of in April as previously planned. 

Looking forward to the full implementation!

Source: https://x.com/vechainofficial/status/1912515090688917899

## HangNDry is listed and looking for endorsements

![[Pasted image 20250421220229.png]]

Sounds promising, good luck on your endorsement hunt!
