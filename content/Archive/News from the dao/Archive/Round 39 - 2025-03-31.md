## BetterBag, VeRecycle and BetterMode implements authentication
Starting round 40 (from 2025-03-31), the team behind @BetterBag_vet, @VeRecycle and @BetterMode will implement authentication for their dApps (BetterMode is still pending endorsements)

From this date, every user will need to either verify a vet.domain with know-your-customer verification, hold a VeChain node or one of their new VeDucks NFTs. The latter even offers a 2x multiplier!

In addition, if you endorse one of their dApps you will get a 1.5 multiplier for the dApp you endorsed. 

Source: https://x.com/BetterBag_vet/status/1904560777811706229 & https://x.com/BetterBag_vet/status/1906652743642550380

#betterbag #verecycle #bettermode

## MeterMate adds a verification stage
@meter_mate has has some issues with their AI scan incorrectly parsing the digits on your meter. With this new update, users will be able to verify the data that they have parsed prior to submitting it. Did it get it right? Send it through. Misread something? Take a new picture.

Source: https://x.com/meter_mate/status/1904897393029767339

## VeLottery goes live with sustainability tickets
@Ve_lottery are now endorsed into the DAO and are live with their first lottery using only sustainability tickets.

To be eligible for free tickets, you must:

- Have participated in at least two apps in the previous round
- Have participated in the allocation voting
- Not been signaled as a bad actor

Each sustainable action counts as one ticket, with a maximum of 10 free tickets available to claim.

Are you feeling lucky?

Source: https://x.com/Ve_lottery/status/1906650913126670496

## ScoopUp adds backup camera options
Like many other dApps, @ScoopUp_vet has had issues with android users struggling to open the camera. They've solved this with a backup camera option whenever you're submitting. 

On top of this they're teasing a V2 of ScoopUp: in-app staking, dynamic rewards with NFT multipliers, X-node and endorser rewards as well as a huge partnership with MVA!
