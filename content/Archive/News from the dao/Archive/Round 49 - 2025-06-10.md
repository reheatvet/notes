## ScoopUp walks with PAWS and launches updates

![[Pasted image 20250610084918.png|500]]

@ScoopUp_vet has had a busy week with their first in person event showcasing their dApp to a bunch of dog owners at the annual **PAWSwalk**. Marketing in action, folks!

In addition, they have finalized their @Groncardx integration meaning their users can now enjoy gift cards to Petco and Petsmart (in addition to all other GronCard gift cards) directly in the ScoopUp dapp.

Further, they've announced that the team members' personally minted @Thorheads has been delegated to the ScoopUp VTHO sponsor account for their users to enjoy **gas-free transactions.** That's a really cool power move! 

Finally - this week their grant proposal is up for the support phase. The grants are supposed to be pushed through to the voting stage by the foundation's grant team but it appears that the vot3s are yet to be deposited. Once it has passed support - **remember to vote for it.**

## Cleanify teases massive app upgrades

@cleanify_vet has revealed a huge list of pending updates to their dApp. Check out the source below for the full details but some highlights include:

- Changing their **manual review** (and by extension the review2earn program) to one based on the @vechainofficial **B3MO AI.** 
- **Clean ups** gets a social overhaul to incentivize cleaning up with friends!
- **Quick drops** are added for folks on the run. Smaller, simple actions should now be easier to complete in the dApp. 
- **Compost all the things!** You can now snap photos of your compost to earn rewards. 
- **Waste sorting and proper cigarette butt discards** will now be rewarded.

It's not yet released - and there are even some more features not yet announced - but this is one dApp I will definitely revisit more moving forward.

**Sources**: https://x.com/cleanify_vet/status/1929977286243041616

## Plans moving forward for EcoBag

@\_BagSolutions\_ are updating their rewards from this week and even tease some upcoming plans.

Their base rewards are scaled down to 5 B3TR per submission, but their EcoBag holders (the phygital NFT - learn more in my [[⭐ Getting Started with dApps]] guide) gets an increased multiplier at 3.1X and any X-Node holder will be increased to 2.4X. 

**BagCoin**, a custom token for the EcoBag project, is coming soon. Users are supposed to earn this by submitting photos, staying active and engaging with the dApp and it will supposedly be used to boost your multiplier further.

Finally, an achievement system for consistent users will be introduced. Users can earn badges with their achievements and can stake them for extra utility. 

**Sources**: https://x.com/_BagSolutions_/status/1930413660842996154
