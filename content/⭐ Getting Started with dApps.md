> [!tip] Voting Multipliers
> 
> Many dApps have integrated a "Voting Multiplier" system. This means that if you voted with your VOT3 tokens in the previous funding round, you will earn a multiplier on your rewards in these apps.
> 
> - **How it works:** The multiplier and the amount of VOT3 required varies by app. Some simply check if you voted at all, while others have tiered thresholds. 
> 	- Most apps support both normal voting through VeBetterDAO as well as veDelegate voting *(including in-app staking in dApps and Lock2Earn terms)* - see [[🚀 The Voter's Guide to the Galaxy]]
>     
> - **Who to vote for:** Generally, it does **not** matter whicNh app you voted for. You just need to have participated in governance.
>     
> 
> Look for the 🗳️ **Voting Multiplier Active** tag on the apps below!

- E-receipts from sustainable stores
- Proof of organic/eco-friendly purchases

## 🥩 veDelegate (Lock2Earn)

🏛️ **VeBetterDAO page:** [https://governance.vebetterdao.org/apps/0x9bc95bbe51b41d526e45466b32a95c2f170a79e74fe31a5782762f7a545e567a](https://governance.vebetterdao.org/apps/0x9bc95bbe51b41d526e45466b32a95c2f170a79e74fe31a5782762f7a545e567a)

veDelegate rewards users through their "Lock2Earn" mechanism. You can lock your B3TR for terms ranging from 28 to 364 days. These tokens cannot be withdrawn or moved during the term.

- **Rewards:** You earn a share of veDelegate's allocation. The longer your lock term and the higher your share of the total pool, the more you earn.
    
- **Voting:** Each locked term creates a separate account on-chain that votes on your behalf. Votes are cast based on the preference set in the main veDelegate dashboard (defaulting to 100% for veDelegate if no preference is set). All terms vote the same way.
    
- **Fees & Yield:** These votes accumulate voting rewards as normal, which are charged with veDelegate's 10% platform fees. The Lock2Earn component itself is not charged the 10% fee; you earn based on the allocation share described above.
    
- **Access:** Once a term closes, rewards are sent to your staking wallet. Importantly, Lock2Earn tokens **CAN** still be used to support proposals even while locked. Any unclaimed voting rewards after closing the term (from last week) will be sent to your staking wallet.
    

#vedelegate

Points are earnt differently based on the action:

- Limit Order: **1.5x** points
- Swap to B3TR: **1.75x** points

- 2nd Place: **5%** of total rewards
- 3rd Place: **3%** of total rewards
- 4th-10th Place: **2%** of total rewards each
- Remaining **68%**: Distributed proportionally based on points (max 1% per user)
