## The base allocation is removed

![[Pasted image 20250602113520.png]]

The proposal to remove the base allocation in favor of a revamped grant program and allocating the weekly B3TR tokens with votes only has been implemented. This week is the first week we will see how that shakes out. 

The max allocation has been increased to ~339K as the 20% cap is now based on the full 1.7M B3TR and not the ~1.21M from previous rounds.

While the early votes show a significant allocation increase to the top two dApps primarily, there are still ~4000 wallets yet to vote. This could significantly change the outcome so it's **more important than ever that you vote on the dApps you think deserve it!**

Plenty of existing and new dApps have already started to submit their proposal previews to discourse so please read through them and give your thoughts here: https://vechain.discourse.group/

Source: https://governance.vebetterdao.org/rounds/49

#VeBetterDAO
