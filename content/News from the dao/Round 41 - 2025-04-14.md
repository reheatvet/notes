## ST3PR shows incredible APY's with updated multipliers

![[Pasted image 20250414113848.png]]

A few weeks back, @ST3PR_VET changed their rewards structure. I missed covering it that week so here's a quick recap:
- The previous 100% multiplier of having a vet.domain with KYC is changed to 25%
- VeHash NFTs are added in its place at a 100% multiplier
- Genesis NFTs are added as a 150% multiplier together with X Nodes

With these updated multipliers, and great work at removing bad actors and farmers from the dApp, ST3PR has shown incredible rewards. 

On April 11th, they calculated the APY for each NFT level at:
- Corgi Rank 1: 47% 
- Corgi Gang 2.0: 636% 
- Genesis: 1220% 
- VeHashes: 3064%

These APY's are calculated by the current floor price of the NFTs and the average of 15K steps daily. The VeHashes truly offer some amazing returns - plus, 2/3rds of the VeHash sale royalties go back as rewards to users with the last third buying and burning the WoV token.

Source: https://x.com/Pacotheeskimo/status/1911068621264011495 & https://x.com/ST3PR_VET/status/1904154598836781254

## SolarWise sells out the first phase of sun tickets

![[Pasted image 20250414135458.png]]

In just ten minutes, @TheSolarWise sold out the first phase (out of 4) of sun ticket sales. The sun ticket grants you 24 hour access to mint your very own solar panel from farm 3 & 4 launching at the end of may 2025. 

The sun ticket is priced at $75 and do not affect the mint price of the solar NFT itself. It's simply a guaranteed way to get access to the sale without waiting for the NFTs to possibly move to the public sale. 

Didn't get one? There's three more phases of sun tickets yet to be launched.  Stay tuned to their socials.

Source: https://x.com/TheSolarWise/status/1910293609980571672
