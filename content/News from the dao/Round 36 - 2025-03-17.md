## Greencart adds automatic Too Good To Go receipts
![[Pasted image 20250317113736.png]]

@GreenCart_vet has added support for automatic scanning of receipts from Too Good To Go!

By linking your email account to GreenCart and making Too Good To Go purchases your receipt will be automatically scanned. 

> [!important] For round 37 only, these receipts reward 40 B3TR each!

*Source: https://x.com/greencart_vet/status/1900545973274779992*

## MeterMate improves the camera experience
@Meter_Mate has significantly revamped the way they interact with your phone's camera. If you had issues launching the camera before or had issues with multiple lenses (ultrawide, telephoto etc.), give it a go and see if your issues are resolved.

*Source: MeterMate telegram*

## ST3PR introduces in-app staking
![[Pasted image 20250317125737.png|300]]
Like many other dApps, @ST3PR_VET has added the ability to stake your B3TR tokens directly through their dApp. By doing so you will delegate your voting rights to Vedelegate, allowing them to vote in your stead. 

If you have setup your vote preference through Vedelegate before staking with ST3PR your previous preference will apply. If you have never staked through Vedelegate, 100% of your votes will go to ST3PR.

*Source: https://x.com/ST3PR_VET/status/1900511112086298905*

## VNS Tokens are sent to Vet.domains owners
Have you received VNS tokens in your wallet? These were sent out to every owner of a @vetDomains. Points have accumulated over the past 12 months and are now distributed as on-chain tokens. 

While they have no announced utility yet, vet.domains says it's coming in the future.

*Source: https://x.com/vetDomains/status/1900218976594256151

## Ecobag releases their Ecobadge NFT's
![[Pasted image 20250317125902.png]]
@_BagSolutions_ has released their Eco Badge collection of NFT's at:  https://worldofv.art/collection/6abb5003-54e0-4257-a84d-ee60c3ef24a0?tab=onsale&page=1&query=&onlyStakeable=0

The collection is a 110 NFT collection of badges that users can redeem for a physical EcoBag with an integrated NFC chip. These NFC bags will be used in their VeBetterDAO dApp (when it's released) to earn their users B3TR rewards when shopping with their dApp.

*Source: https://x.com/_BagSolutions_/status/1899123888384065832*

## Betterbag changes submission requirements
@BetterBag_vet has announced that their weekly maximum submission limit is changed to **3, down from 5, starting round 37 (March 17th 2025)**.

In addition, each receipt **must contain a minimum of 5 items listed on the receipt!** Five items of the same type is accepted. If you want to purchase 5 bottles of mango juice only, go right ahead.

> [!NOTE]
> [[⭐ Getting Started with dApps]] has been updated with this change.

*Source: https://x.com/BetterBag_vet/status/1901087361729007901*
## Velottery launches on mainnet
![[Pasted image 20250317125924.png|300]]
@Velottery has successfully launched on Vechain's mainnet. While their dApp is yet to be endorsed into the #VeBetterDAO, the dApp is up and running with the lottery being funded by purchased tickets. 

Each ticket is currently priced at **10 B3TR with three winners every week.**

*Source: https://x.com/Ve_lottery/status/1901349256977387752*
