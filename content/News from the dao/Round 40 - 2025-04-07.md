## Justote doubles rewards. Runs out in a day
On March 31st, @JusToteDAO announced changes to the submission rules. With 4 submissions per week and each successful submission giving 1 B3TR, users can earn up to 4 B3TR weekly. 

Unfortunately, they had a quick drain with all rewards running out after just a day. While the allocation share is very good at 75/25 overall, they do appear do have some large farming issues going on. Pending more thorough investigation.

Source: https://x.com/JusToteDAO/status/1906702028320977234 & https://x.com/JusToteDAO/status/1907061479028760891

## BetterSwap launches massive updates

![[Pasted image 20250407123907.png]]

@MyVechain have announced two massive updates to their dApp. Firstly, they have completed a full rebrand. New logo, new name, new banner. They are now officially changed from The Better Collective to BetterSwap.

## World of V launches Sustainable Proofs

![[Pasted image 20250407125059.png]]

Want to document your sustainable actions, not just in the dApps that reward you with B3TR, but as a permanent, on-chain record in the form of an  NFT? [^1]  - now you can. 

Open their dApp, browse to the Sustainable Proofs page, add a title and description to your action and snap a picture. By doing so you're minting your very own NFT of the action while entering a raffle for 10 B3TR rewarded to 100 users weekly.

Source: https://x.com/worldofv_art/status/1907159641332887940 & https://medium.com/@WorldofV/sustianble-proof-nfts-onworld-of-v-tokenizing-impactful-actions-3e9592e48eb5
