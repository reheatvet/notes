## The Stable Coin Glodollar joins the DAO
![[Pasted image 20250323133401.png|250]]

@glodollar is officially live on vechain. Glo Dollar is a stable coin tied to the US dollar - for every $1 USDGLO, there's $1 backing it off-chain. That means you can finally swap from B3TR, VET or VTHO to a token that has significantly lower volatility. Plus, all profits they make go back into the VeBetterDAO treasury!

## VeLottery updates their ticket structure and gets endorsed
@Ve_lottery has received feedback about their sustainability angle for their new dApp. They have therefore announced updates to it - for every Better Action you complete in the VeBetterDAO, you will be able to claim tickets to enter the lottery with. A great way to incentivize users to engage with dApps across the DAO!

Shortly after announcing this they were successfully endorsed into the DAO. Users will be able to vote for them starting round 39 with the first lottery with #VeBetterDAO allocations starting on round 40 - March 31st. 

Here are the rules to play:
1. The wallet needs to have voted. 
2. Needs to have used at least two different apps. 
3. cannot be signaled by any dApp. 
4. 1 sustainable action = 1 claimable ticket 
5. Maximum of 10 claimable tickets 6. Claimable tickets are based on the previous weeks data. (So this week matters for next weeks lottery! )

Source: https://twitter.com/Ve_lottery/status/1902096870115037431 & https://twitter.com/Ve_lottery/status/1902266781311393871
