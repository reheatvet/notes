## Galaxy Membership (GM NFT)
The first time you vote you will unlock a galaxy membership (GM) NFT. This nft is your ticket to vote. The first level, 1, is the Earth nft. 

Higher levels can either be: 
- purchased with a [donation of b3tr to the VeBetterDAO treasury](https://docs.vebetterdao.org/vebetterdao/voter-rewards#b3tr-requirements-for-upgrades)
- upgraded for free if you hold a [valid vechain node of strength or above. ](https://docs.vebetterdao.org/vechain-node-holders-guides/vechain-nodes-x-gm-nfts-maximizing-benefits-within-vebetter-dao)

The earth level has **no impact** on rewards. **Moon (level 2)** and above reward you with a share of the GM NFT pool with your weekly voting rewards. 

Learn more here https://docs.vebetterdao.org/vebetterdao/voter-rewards
### Tier 1: Earth
- Free voting access
- No additional rewards

### Tier 2+: Moon & Beyond
- Share in weekly GM NFT reward pool
- Upgrade options:
  - Donate B3TR to DAO treasury
  - Hold Vechain Strength Node (or higher) for free upgrade

## Weekly Voting Cycle
### Snapshot Mechanism
- Occurs weekly after ~23:59 UTC Sunday
- Records VOT3 balance for voting power
- Key rules:
  - Convert B3TR→VOT3 1:1 via official channels only
  - DEX conversions are incompatible
  - Timing may vary slightly week-to-week

### Proof of Participation
- Requirement: 3 Better Actions within last 12 rounds
- Same actions that earn B3TR count toward eligibility

## Voting Methods

### 1. VeBetterDAO (Manual)
- **Features:**
  - 100% voting rewards
  - Full quadratic voting impact
  - Requires personal wallet
- **Process:**
  - Manual B3TR→VOT3 conversion
  - Manual voting
  - Manual reward claims
- **Restrictions:**
  - Incompatible with VeDelegate normal voting
  - No locking requirements

### 2. VeDelegate Normal
- **Features:**
  - 90% voting rewards (10% delegate fee)
  - Full quadratic voting impact
  - Uses personal wallet
- **Automation:**
  - Snapshot handling
  - Voting execution
  - Reward distribution
- **Restrictions:**
  - Incompatible with VeBetterDAO voting

### 3. VeDelegate Lock2Earn
- **Features:**
  - 90% base rewards + bonus yields
  - Full quadratic voting impact
  - Supports up to 3 wallets (1 per term)
- **Locking:**
  - 28-364 day terms
- **Automation:**
  - Full process automation
- **Compatibility:**
  - Works with other methods

## Quadratic Voting System
- Core principle: Many small voters > few large voters
- Example:
  - 1000 wallets with 10 VOT3 > 10 wallets with 1000 VOT3
- Implementation varies by voting method
